About Me

I am a graduate of the Department of Economics, with strong analytical skills and a solid understanding of financial and economic data. My passion for data analysis has driven me to expand my technical expertise and actively pursue a career in Data Analytics.

I have hands-on experience using SQL for data extraction and processing, building basic ETL processes, and designing Data Warehouses with Microsoft SQL Server technologies. I also utilize Power BI to create interactive dashboards and data visualizations that support strategic business decision-making. Additionally, I have worked on projects using Python for data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and automation.

The combination of my analytical mindset, adaptability, and commitment to continuous learning makes me a valuable asset to any team that leverages data to improve performance and outcomes.
